Seasonal Calendars

A cohesive list of the marriage candidate's birthdays by season, tips about which gifts should be given to them, and other yearly events! Note that during some yearly events, marriage candidates may not be around to recieve gifts. The tips will reference friendship points, which if you are unsure what that is, please visit the Friendship System page!

Spring

Important Birthdays & Events

  • Spring 13 - Egg Festival
  • Spring 14 - Haley's Birthday
  • Spring 20 - Shane's Birthday
  • Spring 24 - Flower Dance
  • Spring 27 - Emily's Birthday

Best Birthday Gift(s)

  • The easiest gift to give Haley are the forageable Daffodils. It is a liked gift meaning you will recieve atleast 360 friendship points with an additional percentage depending on the quality.
  • The best gift for Emily are any gems you may have from mining, all giving 640 friendship points.
  • For Shane, the best gift to give him is either the Pizza or Beer you can purchase at the saloon. Both are loved gifts meaning you will recieve 640 friendship points!

Summer

Important Birthdays & Events

  • Summer 10 - Maru's Birthday
  • Summer 11 - Luau
  • Summer 13 - Alex's Birthday
  • Summer 17 - Sam's Birthday
  • Summer 28 - Dance of the Moonlight Jellies

Best Birthday Gift(s)

  • If you have any leftover Cauliflower or Strawberries from Spring, then those are the perfect loved gifts to give to Maru. If not, a Gold Bar is a great option. All these gifts give 640 points but the crops have an additional percentage depending on quality.
  • Alex is not a picky man, but he is a hard person to gift. An easy liked gift would be a gem or a flower to get an easy minimum of 360 friendship points. If you are in year 2, the best gift for him would be the Complete Breakfast which is a recipe you can learn from the TV on Spring 21. Though the recipe is a bit complicated, the ingredients are easily accesible by summer. Giving you a well-deserved 640 friendship points!
  • Sam's best loved gift is Pizza, which is easily boughten from the saloon. This will net you a whopping 640 friendship points!

Fall

Important Birthdays & Events

  • Fall 2 - Penny's Birthday
  • Fall 5 - Elliot's Birthday
  • Fall 13 - Abigail's Birthday
  • Fall 16 - Stardew Valley Fair
  • Fall 27 - Spirit's Eve

Best Birthday Gift(s)

  • Because of how early her birthday is in the week, the best gifts to give Penny are either Melon or a Poppy. These two loved gifts are farmed in summer and can yield upwards of 640 friendships points, with an additional percentage depending on the quality.
  • Make sure you have an ocean crab pot, because Elliot's easiest gifts are Lobster and Crab Cakes! These gifts give 640 friendship points each.
  • Abigail loves to eat Amethysts. Give her an Amethyst to get an easy 640 friendship points.

Winter

Important Birthdays & Events

  • Winter 8 - Festival of Ice
  • Winter 10 - Sebastian's Birthday
  • Winter 14 - Harvey's Birthday
  • Winter 15-17 - Winter Market
  • Winter 23 - Leah's Birthday
  • Winter 25 - Feast of the Winter Star

Best Birthday Gift(s)

  • Sebastian is an easy man, give him either a Frozen Tear or Sashimi to get an easy 640 friendship points.
  • Harvey's easiest loved gift is coffee, which can be bought from the saloon. Give this to him for 640 friendship points.
  • Leah's tastes are a bit rich, despite her lifestyle, thus an easy gift would be almost any forageable. These are liked gifts and give 360 friendship points with an additional percentage depending on the quality.
  • For the Winter Star event, ff by chance your favourite bachelor or bachelorettes turns out to be your secret gift reciever, giving them a loved gift will give you x5 the friendship points than normal!
