Friendship System

Friendship is a mechanic within Stardew Valley which consists of building relationships with the people of Pelican Town. There are various factors to building friendships which the game does not explicitly tell players. The 3 main sub-mechanics that you will learn about are the points meter, gifting, and heart events!

Points Meter

Friendship is calculated as a numerical value which may increase or decrease depending on your actions. Though the exact number is not given in-game, a general representation of a range in friendship is expressed using hearts within the social tab of the menu. Each heart represents 250 friendship points and will appear red for every heart amount reached. Every villager has a maximum of ten achievable hearts, with marriage candidates having only 8 at the start. This amount can be increased later if you decide to pursue them.

Another indicator is during direct interaction with a villager. An icon to the right of the villager's portrait will be a specific color depending on how many hearts you have with them. Each color represents every two hearts, starting at 0.

Gaining Points

You can easily gain points by doing the following:

  • 20 points - Talking to them daily
  • Giving neutral, liked, or loved gifts
  • Picking certain answers during interactive events
  • 150 points - Delivery quests

Losing Points

Be wary of losing points from the following:

  • -2 points~20 points per day - Not talking to them
  • Giving disliked or hated gifts
  • Picking certain answers during interactive events
  • -25 points - Going through a trashcan (Linus doesn't mind)

Gifting

Giving gifts is one of the quickest ways to increase or decrease friendship. You can give each villager a gift per day with a maximum of 2 gifts a week. To see what gifts you have given already, check the gift log located within the villager's page in the social tab menu.

Preference

Each villager has a range of item preferences which may give one of these amounts:

  • 80 points - Love
  • 45 points - Like
  • 20 points - Neutral
  • -20 points - Dislike
  • -40 points - Hate

Item Quality

For certain items, it is possible to obtain a higher quality. Each item equality give an additional percentage:

  • 0% - Normal
  • 10% - Silver
  • 25% - Gold
  • 50% - Iridium

Birthdays and Events

Giving gifts during these events are excluded from the maximum weekly gift cap. Giving a gift on a birthday will multiply the points by 8. Giving a gift during Feast of the Winter Star will multiply the points by 5.

Heart Events

When your friendship with a villager increases, you will unlock an event with that villager. These events happen in specific locations when both you and the villager are in the same loaded area. Some heart events are non-interactive while some gives you options that affect how the scene plays out. Doing so may even raise or lower points so the best course of action is to answer how you believe the villager would agree.
